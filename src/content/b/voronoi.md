---
title: Voronoi
publishDate: 2026-04-05
tags: ["Poetry"]
---

The rattle of applause\
turned ninety degrees into rain,\
then that patter to piss,\
and anxiety to excitement;\

great drums to movements\
that were flushed away\
as I left the town\
square for the wilderness.\

I then ate leaves.\
I ate leaves for 3 years.\
They were everywhere,\
Voronoi tiling my quail-ia.\

Always different,\
And beautiful like we.\
A green snow-storm.\
An organic fractal\

Leafy tendrils plucked another and others.\
Yet this next time, surprised by blue, i saw the sea,\
Then i stopped seeing all together.\
I grew into the sand and felt the sodium increase as i s(l)ipped from my toes.\

I felt the massage of the tides and knew Nirvana.\
Dark balls arrived and congregated around my waist,\
They grew in numbers, seeping into me.\
I felt strange yet happy.\

Pulses of humanity shuddered through me,\
Until.. I lost myself in the transformation.\
